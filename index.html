Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about OctonetAI’s platform, services, and features.

Q1: What is AnonAI?

AnonAI is a private, anonymous AI chat platform that allows you to interact with customizable AI agents. You can create your own agents, engage in uncensored conversations, and explore a wide range of topics—all while prioritizing your privacy and security. Powered by cutting-edge AI models like Hermes 3, Llama 3.1, and FLUX.1, AnonAI offers a unique experience for those seeking creativity, advice, and freedom of expression.

Q2: How can I create and customize AI agents on AnonAI?

Creating and customizing AI agents on AnonAI is easy and flexible. Simply design your own agent by defining its personality, expertise, and conversational style. Once your agent is created, you can choose to keep it private, share it for feedback, or even sell it. With full control over the customization process, AnonAI allows you to shape agents to suit your unique needs and preferences.

Q3: What AI models does AnonAI offer?

AnonAI offers access to a variety of advanced AI models, including Hermes 3 by Nous Research, Llama 3.1 405B, and Llama 3.2 Vision by Meta. These models are designed for diverse applications, from text generation and creative writing to image creation and personal advice. Additionally, AnonAI users can create and personalize their own AI agents tailored to specific needs.

Q4: How does AnonAI ensure security?

AnonAI ensures your privacy and security by storing all data solely on your device with end-to-end encryption. There are no accounts, logins, or tracking, meaning your conversations are fully anonymous. AnonAI is designed to prioritize user confidentiality, keeping your personal information and chat data secure at all times.

Q5: Can I use my custom AI agents for different projects on AnonAI?

Yes, AnonAI allows you to use your custom AI agents for a variety of personal or professional projects. Whether you’re working on creative writing, business strategy, or personal development, you can tailor your AI agents to fit the needs of each project, providing unique insights and assistance along the way.

The typical turnaround time varies based on the subscription tier. For our Casual tier, standard projects are completed within a few business days. Pro tier clients enjoy expedited delivery, ensuring high-priority tasks are addressed swiftly and efficiently.

How do I submit a design request, and what information is needed?

Design requests can be submitted through our user-friendly client portal. You'll need to provide detailed project requirements, including any relevant assets or brand guidelines, to ensure we deliver designs that meet your expectations and drive your vision forward.

Payments for both Casual and Pro tiers are made monthly via a secure online platform. Clients are billed at the beginning of each subscription cycle, ensuring uninterrupted access to our top-tier design and development services.

Absolutely. We understand that business needs evolve, and you can upgrade or downgrade your subscription plan at any time. Simply reach out to our support team, and we'll make the necessary adjustments to your account promptly.

What kind of customer support is available to me?

We pride ourselves on exceptional customer support tailored to your subscription tier. Casual tier clients receive reliable email support, while Pro tier clients benefit from priority support, along with bi-weekly strategy sessions to keep your projects on track and aligned with your goals.
